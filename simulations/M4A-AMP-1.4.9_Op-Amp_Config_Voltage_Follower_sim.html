M4A-AMP-1.4.9 — Voltage Follower Configuration

Phase margin vs capacitive load, step response with slewing, and impedance transformation

Phase Margin vs Capacitive Load

The load capacitor creates a pole that degrades phase margin. Unity-gain follower is most sensitive. Adding R_iso (series at output, outside feedback) moves the pole to higher frequency, restoring stability.

Voltage Follower Step Response

For small steps (within linear range), settling is exponential. For large steps, slewing dominates first, then exponential settling follows. The total time = t_slew + t_settle. Drag step size to see the transition.

Impedance Transformation vs Loop Gain

Zin,cl = Zcm × (1+Avol). Zout,cl = Zout_open / (1+Avol). Both transform by the same factor. A 10,000× increase in Avol gives 10,000× higher Zin and 10,000× lower Zout — simultaneously.
