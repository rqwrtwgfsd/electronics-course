Capacitive Load Stability: Phase Margin vs Cload

A capacitive load with Zout,open creates an additional pole at fp = 1/(2π·Zout·CL). When this pole falls below GBW, phase margin collapses — causing oscillation. Snubber Riso pushes the pole higher.
