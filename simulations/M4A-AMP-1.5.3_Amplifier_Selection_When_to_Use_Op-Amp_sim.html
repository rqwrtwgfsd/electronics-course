Gain Stability vs Temperature: CE vs CS vs Op-Amp

CE gain ∝ 1/T (absolute temperature). CS gain ∝ µn ∝ T^-1.5. Op-amp gain is resistor ratio — < 10 ppm/°C. The three-way comparison shows why op-amps dominate precision analog design.

Input Bias Current Error vs Source Impedance

Vbias_error = IB × Rs. Each curve shows a different input type. CMOS op-amp (pA IB) is essential above 100 kΩ source. BJT op-amp (nA) limits at 10 kΩ. CE BJT (µA) limits at 1 kΩ. CS MOSFET is ideal for high-Z.
