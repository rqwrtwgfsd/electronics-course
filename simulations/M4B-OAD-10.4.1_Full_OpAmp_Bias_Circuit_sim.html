ISS vs Temperature (with PTAT/CTAT components)

Total ISS
PTAT component
CTAT component
A pure resistor-based reference is CTAT (current decreases with T as resistance rises). Adding a PTAT component (e.g., from VT = kT/q varying with temperature) partially cancels the temperature coefficient. PTAT fraction = 1 means the full reference is PTAT. Fraction = 0 means pure CTAT (resistor-dominated). Setting the fraction to cancel the TC gives a near-zero TC reference current — the analog equivalent of a bandgap.

VDD Ramp and Bias Node Startup Transient

VDD (V)
Bias node voltage
I_ref (normalized)
The bias node starts at 0V (zero-current state). When VDD rises above the startup threshold (Vth of the startup MOSFET), M_start activates and drives a small current into the bias node. The bias node rises with τ_startup = Cgate × R_startup. The op-amp is not fully functional until the bias node reaches >95% of its final value (≥5τ). A fast startup is critical in systems that power up frequently (IoT devices, wake-from-sleep).
