Common-Centroid Layout Visualization

The A-B-B-A / B-A-A-B pattern interleaves M1 (A) and M2 (B) sub-cells symmetrically. Each color-shaded cell shows the Vth at its position (gradient from blue = low to red = high). The average Vth of all M1 cells equals the average of all M2 cells exactly for a linear gradient — systematic mismatch is fully cancelled. Residual error is second-order and proportional to gradient × span².
