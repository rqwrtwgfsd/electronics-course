CMRR has two independent floors: tail source (2g_m × r_SS) and mismatch (V_ov/ΔV_th). The total CMRR is the minimum of the two. At high frequency, the tail capacitance C_SS adds a −20 dB/dec roll-off starting at f_p = 1/(2π r_SS C_SS).

CMRR vs Frequency

Blue: tail-limited CMRR (flat at DC, rolls off above f_p due to C_SS). Orange: mismatch floor (frequency-independent). Green: total CMRR = min(tail, mismatch). The dominant limitation at each frequency is the lower curve.
