The 5T OTA integrator settling is: Vout(t) = Vin × (1 − e^(−t/τ)) where τ = CL/gm1. For a pipeline ADC stage: settling must complete to the required accuracy in T/2 (half clock period). The gain error from finite OTA gain: ε = CS/(CI × Av). This sets the maximum ADC resolution.

CM-OTA effective Gm = (K+1)/2 × gm1. GBW scales linearly with K+1. Noise factor = (1 + K×gm3/gm1) — noise increases sub-linearly with K. Net SNR improvement: 20·log10(GBW_cm/noise_cm) − 20·log10(GBW_5t/noise_5t). Diminishing returns beyond K=3–4: noise grows faster than GBW for large K.
