Gain Error vs Open-Loop Gain

Fractional gain error ε = 1/(1+T) = Acl_ideal / (Avol_dc + Acl_ideal). Each curve is a different closed-loop gain. Higher Acl demands more Avol for the same error floor.

Closed-Loop THD vs Loop Gain T

THD_cl = THD_ol / (1+T). The open-loop distortion is a fixed property of the op-amp internals. High T makes it irrelevant — until T decreases with frequency. Drag the frequency to see T shrink.
