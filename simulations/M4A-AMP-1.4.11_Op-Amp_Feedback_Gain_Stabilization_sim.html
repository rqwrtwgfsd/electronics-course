Closed-Loop Gain Convergence to Ideal as Avol Rises

Acl = Avol/(1+Avol×β). As Avol rises, Acl converges to 1/β. Each curve shows a different target gain. The shaded region shows ±1% from ideal. Below a threshold Avol, gain error exceeds 1%.

Gain Error Budget: Avol Term + Resistor Term

Total gain error = |Acl_real – Acl_ideal| / Acl_ideal. This combines the finite Avol error (1/(1+T)) and the resistor ratio error (set by the slider). Which dominates depends on the system.

Gain Drift vs Temperature (Resistor TC Mismatch)

ΔAcl/Acl ≈ (R2/R1 × ΔTC × ΔT) / Acl. Resistor TC mismatch is the dominant drift mechanism once Avol is large. Matched pairs outperform individual resistors by 10–50×.
