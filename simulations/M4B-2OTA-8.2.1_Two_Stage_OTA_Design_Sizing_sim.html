Performance Verification

SR = ISS/Cc. For a given ISS (which sets gm1 and thus GBW), SR and GBW are coupled. Higher GBW needs higher gm1 → higher ISS → higher SR. But Cc sets GBW independently of ISS via GBW=gm1/2πCc. Increasing Cc reduces GBW without changing SR. Decreasing Cc increases GBW and reduces SR.
