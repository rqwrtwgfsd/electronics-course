Key Design Equations

gm1 = GBW × 2π × CL
ISS = gm1 × Vov (long-channel) or ISS = gm1 × nVT (subthreshold)
W/L = 2×ID / (µCox × Vov²)
ro = 1 / (λ × L_eff⁻¹ × ID) = L / (λ_sheet × ID)
Rout_cascode = gm_c × ro_c × ro_main
Cc_Miller = gm1 / (2π × GBW)
gm2_min = 1.73 × GBW × 2π × CL (for 60° PM)
SR = ISS / CL
FoM = GBW [MHz] × CL [pF] / IDD [mA] → GHz·pF/A
Noise = √(8kT × 2/(3gm1)) at room temperature ≈ √(44/gm1 [mA/V]) nV/√Hz
