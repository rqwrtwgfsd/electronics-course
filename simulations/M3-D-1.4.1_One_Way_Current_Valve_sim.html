M3-D-1.4.1 — Diode as One-Way Current Valve

V_out: 0.00 V
I_D: 0.00 mA
State: OFF
P_diode: 0.00 mW

Diode conducts when V_in exceeds V_F threshold. Output follows input minus the diode drop. Negative input: diode blocks — V_out = 0.

I-V curve showing the switch-like behavior: flat at 0 for negative V, abrupt turn-on at V_F.
