Gain Budget Waterfall Chart

The waterfall chart shows each stage's gain contribution building up to the total. Stage 1 is limited by gm1·Rout1. Stage 2 by gm6·Rout2. The output buffer contributes ~0 dB. Increase Rout by using cascode loads. Increase gm by increasing bias current (costs power). If total gain is still insufficient, consider a gain-boosted cascode or a third gain stage.
