Cascade Bandwidth vs Number of Stages

Splitting a fixed total gain across N stages improves bandwidth. More stages = more BW, but the gain per stage decreases and cascade shrinkage (0.644×, 0.510×...) partially offsets the gain. Optimal N depends on the target total gain.
