Synchronous rectification uses a MOSFET (R_on = milliohms) instead of a diode to eliminate V_F loss. Essential for high-current, low-voltage supplies like CPU power (1.0 V, 100 A).

Power loss comparison as current increases: Diode loss = constant V_F × I; Sync loss = I² × R_on (grows faster at high current).
