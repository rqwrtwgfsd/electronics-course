M4A-AMP-1.4.10 — Negative Feedback Benefits

Gain stabilization, distortion reduction, and impedance transformation — all proportional to loop gain T

Five Feedback Benefits vs Loop Gain T

Every benefit improves by 1/(1+T) or (1+T)×. Gain error, THD, Zout all divide by (1+T). Zin, CMRR, PSRR multiply by (1+T). They all improve on the same diagonal line.

Distortion Reduction vs Frequency

THD_cl(f) = THD_ol / (1+T(f)). T(f) = GBW/(f×Acl). At low f, T is large and THD is tiny. As f rises, T falls and THD_cl approaches THD_ol. The crossover is the "distortion corner."
