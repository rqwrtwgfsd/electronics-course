Blue=magnitude with Rs. Green=phase with Rs. Red dashed=magnitude without Rs. The RHP zero (Rs=0) causes phase to drop past −270°. As Rs approaches 1/gm2, the zero moves to infinity and the phase recovers. Rs > 1/gm2 creates a LHP zero (phase boost).
Zero frequency vs Rs (normalized to 1/gm2). At Rs=0: RHP zero at gm2/(2πCc). At Rs=1/gm2: zero moves to ∞. At Rs>1/gm2: LHP zero reappears, moving lower as Rs increases. Crossing Rs=1/gm2 is the transition from RHP to LHP.
